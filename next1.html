Types of digital computers

The digital computers used today can be grouped into four types. These are:

  1. Micro Computers
  2. MiniComputers
  3. Mainframe Computers
  4. Super Computers

Micro Computers (Personal Computers)

It is a type of computer used by an individual. This computer uses a microprocessor chip called central Processing Unit (CPU) to process instructions. Examples are:

  • laptop
  • Mobile phones
  • Desktop
  • Smart TVs
  • Smart Watches
  • Tablets

    • Uses of Micro Computers

    1. It is used for entertainment
    2. It is used for teaching and learning
    3. It is used for research
    4. It helps to communicate
    5. It is used in hospitals

    Types of Micro Computers

    • Desktop computers
    • Game consoles
    • Laptops
    • Notebooks
    • Palmtop computers
    • Tablet computers

    Mini Computers

    Mini computers are small computers that are used for home and office purposes. They are larger than micro computers and are used for gaming, education, and scientific research.

    Characteristics of Mini Computers

    1. It has higher processing power and storage capacity than micro computers
    2. It has less processing and storage capacity than Mainframe and Super computer
    3. It is smaller than Mainframe and Super computers
